## 💎 THE MANIFESTO
At Kora, we focus on **Refining Minimalism**. Our process is a study in silence—stripping away the unnecessary to reveal the essence of form. Every piece is a testament to our commitment to quality, structure, and contemporary poise.

### 🌑 THE COLLECTIONS
*   **Project 01: Silence** — Our inaugural study. A collection focused on the interplay of shadow, texture, and refined volume.
*   **A/W 2026: Autumn Minimal** — Heavyweight essentials designed to anchor the transitional wardrobe.
*   **The House Basics** — The foundation. High-density fabrics meet precision-engineered cuts.

### ✨ PRODUCT ARCHITECTURE
*   **Upper House**: Essential Oversized Tees, Heavyweight Hoodies, and Monolith Knitwear.
*   **Lower House**: Structured Wide-Leg Trousers and Refined Utility Cargoes.
*   **The Blueprint**: Precision-sourced 100% cotton, heavyweight knits, and matte hardware.
